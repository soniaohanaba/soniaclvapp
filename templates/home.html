About App

This web application was built as a channel to enable business owners monitor the growth of their business and channel their marketing/advertising budgets to the right set of customers The app helps in:

  • Business customers description
  • Customers segmentation using clustering algorithms
  • Customer lifetime value prediction using machine learning models

This app was built by Sharon Okeakwalam. She is a 24 year-old Master's Student in Big Data Management and Analysis. This application was built as part of her thesis project

Start finding insights in your customer data by following these steps

  • 01 Upload your customer data in any of the following file formats (json, csv, xlsx)

    We receive your data, clean it and store it on our system.

  • 02 Start describing your customers using dynamic graphs

    You can visualize your customer data using dynamic graphs. It enables you to see monthly customer transactions, your revenue, revenue fluctuations over a benchmark etc.

  • 03 Use our RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) values clustering descriptions to segment your customers

    We cluster your customer data and enable you to visualize it based on different parameters.

 

Services

The capabilities of this app are as follows

Customers description

Visualize your customer data and gain insights from it using dynamic graphs

Customer segmentation/clustering

Group your customers using clustering algorithms so as to know who to spend your marketing budget on

Prediction

Predict the lifetime value of your customers using the power of machine learning.
