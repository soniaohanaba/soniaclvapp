About App

This web application was built as a channel to enable business owners monitor the growth of their business and channel their marketing/advertising budgets to the right set of customers The app helps in:

  • Business customers description
  • Customers segmentation using clustering algorithms
  • Customer lifetime value prediction using machine learning models

This app was built by Sharon Okeakwalam. She is a 24 year-old Master's Student in Big Data Management and Analysis. This application was built as part of her thesis project
